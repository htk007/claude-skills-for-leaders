# 📦 Product & Project Manager Skills

> Claude skills designed for Product and Project Managers — the people responsible for defining what gets built, why it matters, and how the work gets delivered.

PMs and Project Managers produce a constant stream of written output: requirements, updates, prioritization frameworks, planning docs. The skills in this folder help you produce that output faster — and at a higher standard.

## 👤 Who This Is For

You'll get the most out of these skills if you:

- Own a product backlog or project plan
- Write requirements, specs, or updates regularly
- Facilitate sprint ceremonies or stakeholder reviews
- Spend too much time on the "how do I word this" part of your job
- Want Claude to structure your thinking, not replace it

---

## 💡 Product Manager vs Project Manager

Both roles are covered here. The distinction matters for some skills:

- **Product Manager** focus: user stories, PRDs, prioritization, roadmap communication
- **Project Manager** focus: sprint planning, stakeholder updates, risk tracking, delivery summaries

Where it matters, each skill notes which role it's optimized for — and offers variations for the other.
